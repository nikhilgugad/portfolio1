About Me

I am a front-end developer and graphic designer with experience in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as front-end frameworks like React and Vue.js. I specialize in logo and icon design using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, and I have strong skills in video editing and motion graphics with Premiere Pro and After Effects. I am also proficient in MS Office and can type at 70 words per minute. I am passionate about creating visually appealing and functional digital experiences. Based in Kuchaman City, Rajasthan, India, I’m always eager to learn and grow in both design and development.


Hobbies and Interests

When I'm not coding, you can often find me on the cricket field, engaging in fast bowling or strategizing my next move in a game of chess. These activities bring balance, excitement, and a sense of discipline to my life. Cricket not only keeps me physically active but also sharpens my reflexes, endurance, and teamwork skills, as it’s a sport that demands quick decision-making and coordination with teammates. It’s a great way to stay fit and relieve stress after long hours of coding. On the other hand, chess is a game that stimulates my mind and hones my problem-solving abilities. The strategic thinking involved in chess helps me approach challenges with a more analytical mindset, which translates well into my work as a developer and designer. Both of these hobbies complement each other, as cricket keeps me agile and focused, while chess strengthens my ability to think several steps ahead. Together, they help me maintain a well-rounded and balanced lifestyle.
